[N99] Seonbi Scholars, Chunhyang & Jeonju

About This Trip

A 2-day cultural journey through three of Korea's most storied cities. Start in Yeongju with the Seonbi Culture Festival — celebrating Korea's Confucian scholarly heritage at one of the nation's oldest Buddhist temples. Move on to Namwon for an evening at the legendary Chunhyang Festival, one of Korea's most beloved folk celebrations. Spend the night in the mountains near Jirisan, then finish with a full morning in Jeonju's iconic Hanok Village. Government-sponsored for international students.

Key Experiences

  • Buseoksa Temple — one of Korea's oldest wooden structures, dating to the Silla period
  • Yeongju Seonbi Culture Festival — celebrating the Confucian scholar (seonbi) tradition
  • Namwon Chunhyang Festival (evening) — Korea's most romantic folk festival
  • Overnight at Jirisan Youth Camp, Namwon
  • Morning rural village experience & breakfast
  • Namwon Municipal Kim Byeong-jong Art Museum
  • Wonseok Myeonghwa Village — folk painting experience & lunch
  • Jeonju Hanok Village & Gyeonggijeon Royal Shrine
  • National Jeonju Museum & Jeonju History Museum
