Seoul Invites You! International Student Meetup at Gwanghwamun Plaza

About This Event

Kim's Community House hosted an exclusive afternoon at the Everyone's Companion Festival in Gwanghwamun Plaza — an outdoor market celebrating local Korean artisans and small businesses. We brought together international and exchange students from across Seoul for a shared afternoon of culture, food, and community.

Attendees explored the K-Made Market, browsed handcrafted goods from local vendors, caught live busking performances, and experienced traditional Korean games. Every participant received a free 20,000 KRW Starbucks coupon just for showing up. The day wrapped up with a group dinner and party.

Highlights

  • K-Made Market — handmade goods from Seoul's independent artisans
  • Live busking performances at Gwanghwamun Square
  • K-Traditional Games (3 types)
  • Free 20,000 KRW Starbucks coupon for all attendees ☕
  • Group dinner & party after the festival
  • Meeting international students from around the world

About the Festival

The Everyone's Companion Festival (동행축제 2026) is a Korean government-sponsored market held at Gwanghwamun Square, Yukjo Yard. The festival supports local small businesses with K-Beauty, K-Made, and K-Lifestyle product booths, alongside cultural performances and prize events.
