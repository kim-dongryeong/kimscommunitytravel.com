[N96] Jeongseon Arirang Festival

About This Trip

The Jeongseon Arirang Festival celebrates one of Korea's most beloved folk songs — designated as UNESCO's Intangible Cultural Heritage in 2012. The festival has run since 1976 and continues to expand its collection of over 5,000 song lyrics.

What's Included

  • Free lunch
  • Free beer at the festival 🍺
  • English-speaking tour guide
  • All entrance and experience fees
