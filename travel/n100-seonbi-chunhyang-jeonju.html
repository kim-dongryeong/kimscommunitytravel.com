[N100] Seonbi Scholars, Chunhyang & Jeonju

About This Trip

A 2-day cultural journey through three of Korea's most storied cities. Start in Yeongju (영주) with the Seonbi Culture Festival — celebrating Korea's Confucian scholarly heritage at one of the nation's oldest Buddhist temples. Move on to Namwon (남원) for an evening at the legendary Chunhyang Festival, one of Korea's most beloved folk celebrations. Spend the night in the mountains near Jirisan, then finish with a full morning in Jeonju's (전주) iconic Hanok Village. Government-sponsored for international students.

Key Experiences

  • Buseoksa Temple (부석사)VisitKorea — one of Korea's oldest wooden structures, dating to the Silla period · UNESCO World Heritage
  • Yeongju Seonbi Culture Festival (영주 한국선비문화축제)VisitKorea — celebrating the Confucian scholar (선비) tradition
  • Namwon Chunhyang Festival (남원 춘향제)VisitKorea — Korea's most romantic folk festival, 96th edition
  • Overnight at Jirisan Youth Camp (지리산유스캠프), Namwon
  • Morning rural village experience & breakfast
  • Namwon Municipal Kim Byeong-jong Art Museum (남원시립 김병종미술관)Wikipedia
  • Wonsaek Painting Village (원색명화마을)Official — folk painting experience & lunch
  • Jeonju Hanok Village (전주 한옥마을)VisitKorea & Gyeonggijeon Royal Shrine (경기전)VisitKorea
  • National Jeonju Museum (국립전주박물관) & Jeonju History Museum (전주역사박물관)
