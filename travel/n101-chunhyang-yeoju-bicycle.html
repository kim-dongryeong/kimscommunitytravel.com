[N101] Chunhyang Festival & Yeoju Bicycle Festival

About This Trip

A 2-day Golden Week getaway combining two of Korea's most enjoyable spring festivals. Spend Day 1 at the full Chunhyang Festival (춘향제) in Namwon (남원) — including the grand parade — and stay overnight at a rural village for a genuine countryside experience. On Day 2, head to Yeoju (여주) for a scenic bicycle festival, then wrap up in Seoul with the city's famous Spring Festa (서울스프링페스타). Government-sponsored for international students.

Key Experiences

  • Namwon Chunhyang Festival (남원 춘향제)VisitKorea & grand street parade — 96th edition, Korea's oldest folk festival
  • Namwon Municipal Kim Byeong-jong Art Museum (남원시립 김병종미술관)Wikipedia
  • Overnight at a rural village (농촌체험마을) — traditional dinner & countryside experience
  • Morning breakfast at the rural village
  • Yeoju Traditional Market (여주시장)
  • Yeoju Bicycle Festival (여주자전거페스티벌) — cycling by the Han River
  • Seoul Spring Festa (서울스프링페스타)Official site — drone shows, K-pop & Han River · free admission
