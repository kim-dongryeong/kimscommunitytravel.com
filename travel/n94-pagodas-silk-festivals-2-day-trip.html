[N94] Pagodas, Silk & Festivals

About This Trip

A 2-day journey through central Korea visiting ancient pagodas, a living silk tradition, and one of the world's most unique hat festivals. Government-sponsored to support local cultural tourism.

Visiting Sites

  • Botapsa Temple — one of Korea's tallest stone pagodas, standing 13 stories high
  • Jincheon Nongdari Bridge — Korea's oldest stone footbridge, still standing after 1,000 years
  • Mir 309 Bridge — Korea's longest 309 m suspension bridge
  • Saenggeo Jincheon Cultural Festival — local entertainment and food
  • Hamchang Myeongju Textile Factory — Korea's 500-year silk-making tradition
  • Sangju World Hat Festival — the world's only hat festival with headwear from 50+ countries

What's Included

  • Private bus transportation (round trip)
  • 2 meals
  • All entrance and experience fees
