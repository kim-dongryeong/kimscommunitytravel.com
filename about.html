Our Story

Built for students, by someone who gets it

Kim's Community Travel was founded to solve a real problem: exchange students in Korea want to explore the country but face language barriers, high prices, and tourist-only itineraries.

KCT works directly with Korean government-sponsored cultural programs, which means you get full 2-day trips — private bus, accommodation, meals, entrance fees — for as little as 7 EUR. That's not a discount. That's the actual price.

Every trip is designed exclusively for foreigners and international students living in Korea. No Korean language needed, no awkward group dynamics. Just a big crew of people from all over the world exploring Korea together.
