About the House

Live with other exchange students

Kim's Community House is a shared residence in Seoul designed specifically for foreigners and exchange students. It's more than just accommodation — it's a community of people from all over the world living together in Korea.

The house is connected to KCT, so living here means easy access to all upcoming trips, events, and discounts. You'll also be surrounded by people who speak your language (or at least English) and are going through the same adventure as you.
