Famous Chuseok Camping Party Trip (3-Day)

About This Trip

Our legendary annual Chuseok trip to Korea's east coast — this is the one everyone talks about for years. Hike Seoraksan, camp on the beach, party under the stars, and go surfing. Three days of pure Korea at its most beautiful.

Itinerary

Day 1 — Oct 3

Seoraksan mountain hiking with temple visit → travel to east coast beach → camping setup → beach party

Day 2 — Oct 4

Surfing → beach club → music station → leisure time on the beach

Day 3 — Oct 5

Morning beach time → cleanup at 15:00 → return to Seoul in the evening

  • Volleyball & badminton equipment
